Click on the engineering disciplines you are interested in below. The table will show you which subjects are needed to qualify for all the disciplines you selected. If a subject is available in multiple semesters, you can choose which semester you would prefer to take it in.

If you have selected less that four subjects per semester, you must select courses to fill them. A second science course such as PHYS102, CHEM112 or COSC122 is a good idea to keep a pathway open into a related BSc major. Other popular choices are management or geological sciences but you may take courses from other science, forestry, arts, humanities, health, education or law subjects.

The table assumes that you have achieved University Entrance and some subjects will assume that you have taken a specific subject at NCEA Level 3 (e.g. PHYS101 requires 14 credits in Level 3 Physics).
If you have/are expecting an Excellent Endorsement in any subject, consult Student Advisors as you may be eligible for a Modified Intermediate.

Note that some engineering disciplines have similar/the same requirements. If you did not select a discipline but it is highlighted, this means that with the current subject selection you are also eligible for this discipline.
