Engineering at UC

Click on the engineering disciplines you are interested in. The table will show you which subjects are needed to qualify for all the disciplines you selected.
If a subject is available in multiple semesters, you can choose which semester you would prefer to take it in (although some subjects will have prerequisites).
Subjects will be highlighted in yellow if you have selected more than the recommended four subjects per semester.
If you select papers that cover requirements for disciplines which you did not select, they will also be highlighted.

Note: ENGR100 is an Academic Writing Assessment and is an EFTS free, zero-fee course.
